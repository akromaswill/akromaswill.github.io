Jerome Rafael is an earth based human being. located in switzerland, he expresses himself through fashion and art.

his work's main focus is a harmonic balance between nature and technology as well as staying grounded while being connected to the inner core.

he is building a world where nature and technology do not fight, but merge. inspired by the craftsmanship of ancient civilizations and the visual language of science fiction, he asks: what if humanity once understood something we have since forgotten? working with upcycled and natural materials, he believes that what humanity has already created contains everything needed to build what comes next.

in an age of black screens, rubber soles and concrete walls, reconnecting to nature is not nostalgic, it is necessary. staying grounded by embracing nature's wisdom, is the foundation to reach higher and eventually grow wings. his practice is built beyond duality. if light or dark, digital or organic, past or future, all you need is to remember yourself. and he is inspiring you to see it, by helping you grow your roots and wings.
